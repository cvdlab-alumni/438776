Description of the work:

In this work, starting from a single "diagram" (a 3-array of 3D blocks), I proceeded to detail a 3D model of the place where I live, according to the method of mapping a new diagram in a cell of a "master" diagram.
To carry out this work were used Python, Pyplasm and LAR-CC (especially sysml.py module).
